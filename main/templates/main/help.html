Welcome to the help page! Here are some instructions to help you use this website:

  • Home Page: Navigate to the home page to see an overview of the available features.
  • Upload Excel File: Use the upload section on the home page to upload your Excel file. Make sure the file is in .xls or .xlsx format. Need a template? Download Excel Template
  • View Data: After uploading the file, you can view the processed data by navigating to the respective sections such as Regional Governance, Market Conditions, and Consumer Perception.
  • Calculate Contribution: Go to the Regional Overview page to calculate your contribution. This will unlock the RE-RMI Summary feature.
  • Logout: Click the Sign Out button in the header to log out of your account.
  • Help: If you need assistance, you can always come back to this help page.

If you encounter any issues or have any questions, please contact support at ar.arathysdarsan@gmail.com.
